1. **Introduction**: ALTERMAR LIMITED believes in being clear and open about how it collects and use data related to you. In the spirit of transparency, this policy provides detailed information about how and when financesculpting.com uses cookies and other similar technologies. 
<br/><br/>
2. **What are cookies?**: Cookies are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer or mobile device when you visit certain websites. They allow a site to recognize your device on subsequent visits. 
<br/><br/>
3. **How does financesculpting.com use cookies?**:  financesculpting.com uses cookies to enhance your experience of its website. For example, cookies can remember your preferences, recognize you on your next visit, and can keep you logged in. financesculpting.com also uses cookies to analyze, improve and monitor the use of its services, to tailor its advertising and understand interactions with its marketing and online ads on third party sites, to enforce the terms of use and to prevent fraud.
<br/><br/>
4. **What types of cookies does financesculpting.com use?**:  financesculpting.com uses two types: session cookies, which are temporary and disappear after you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which stay on your device until you delete them or they expire. 
<br/><br/>
5. **How to control and delete cookies**: If you want to block the cookies financesculpting.com sets, you can do so through your browser settings. If you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit the site. Please note that some services and functionalities may not work if you disable the cookies financesculpting.com uses.  For example, essential cookies are strictly necessary for the provision of the services. Advertising cookies use information about your use of this and other websites and your response to ads and emails. Advertising Partners which advertise financesculpting.com services may receive information as part of the campaign targeting in order, among others, to judge the effectiveness of the campaigns.
<br/><br/>
6. **Changes to the Cookie Policy**: financesculpting.com may update this policy from time to time. If financesculpting.com makes significant changes, it will let you know through the website or through other communication channels.
